About

Tech Heist 1.0

India is now ranked among the top three start-up-friendly ecosystems in the world. Programs like Atal Innovation Mission, PM Research Fellowships, Start-up India Abhiyaan, Aatmanirbhar Bharat, SAMRIDHI scheme etc. are the foundations to establish India as a global epicenter of innovation. Both state and central governments have been emphasizing the need to encourage Innovation and Incubation. It is the First Hackathon we are organizing National Level which is held in 20 Hrs.
It is a non-stop product development competition, where problem statements are posed to technology students for innovative solutions.

In this first of it's kind hackathon, teams will register, be shortlisted and finally be invited to build and present their concepts & creations to a panel of judges representing the industries Persons at an exclusive event in March 2023 in Marathwada Mitra Mandal's Institute of Teechnology, Pune,India.

Theme

Sustainable and innovative solutions for industrial infrastructure development.
